My name is Josh Haug and I am a junior Media Arts & Design Major with an Interactive Design Concentration and a minor in Writing, Rhetoric, and Technical Communication.


Ad Campaigns

Throughout my time at JMU, I've created several mockups and designs for ad campaigns across several mediums of media.

Adagio House

Along with the help of some fellow SMAD students, I created several posts for Adagio House, a nonprofit counseling agency based in downtown Harrisonburg. They offer caregiving services to people with physical and mental illnesses, and host various events to raise awareness and increase donations.

Since Adagio House's mission is centered on recovery and rehabilitation, I wanted to use a calm color scheme and font style for this ad campaign. These posts were created for Facebook, while others in my group created content for Instagram and LinkedIn.

Piero's Corner

For SMAD 201, I created an ad campaign for an Italian restaurant called Piero's Corner. The mockups included a menu, business cards, a sticker that can be placed on a wine glass, and an updated logo.

Ole School Alumni Group

I created a multimedia ad campaign for a scholarship group here at JMU. The Ole School Alumni Group is a scholarship organization that gives scholarships to minority students to increase the minority enrollment to JMU. I utilzied print, social, and digital media to complete this ad campaign.

Movie Posters

I created posters and fliers for Reel Change Film Festival, an annual event held here on campus. This festival showcases short films and other projects created by JMU students.
